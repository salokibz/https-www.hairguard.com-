The science

Hair loss follows a predictable pattern

Thinning hair progresses in a well-known sequence called the Norwood Scale. Although the rate of hair loss varies between people, the typical timeframe for someone to go from early thinning to significant baldness is around 15 years.

The good news: hair loss isn't inevitable. At every stage of the Norwood Scale, there are evidence-based interventions that can slow, stop, and — in many cases — reverse the process without drugs or surgery.

The key is acting early. Hairguard's system targets the three primary mechanisms of androgenetic hair loss: reduced scalp blood flow, follicle miniaturisation driven by DHT, and oxidative stress at the follicle level.

  • How long before I see results?

    Most customers notice reduced shedding within 4–6 weeks. Visible regrowth typically appears between months 3 and 6, depending on hair loss stage and consistency of use. The full compounding effect continues to build over 6–12 months.

  • Are there any side effects?

    All Hairguard products are entirely drug-free and designed for zero systemic side effects. Unlike finasteride, our solutions work through physical stimulation and topically-applied natural compounds — safe for long-term daily use for both men and women.

  • Does it work for women too?

    Absolutely. Female hair thinning affects approximately 40–45% of women by age 50. The Hairguard system targets the same underlying mechanisms — reduced scalp circulation and follicle miniaturisation — making it equally effective for women.

  • What is the subscription and how does it work?

    The Caffeine Shampoo and Maxoxidil serum are available on subscription. You save 15% on your first order and 15% on every order after that. Deliveries arrive automatically every 2 months. You can skip or cancel at any time — no lock-ins, and you receive automatic reminders before each renewal.

  • Do I need all the products?

    Each product works independently and many customers start with just the Caffeine Shampoo or the Growband Pro. The products are designed to work synergistically — combining mechanical stimulation with topical actives and microneedling amplifies results significantly. Start with what fits your budget and build from there.

  • How does the Dermaroller increase effectiveness?

    The 192 medical-grade titanium microneedles create micro-channels in the scalp that dramatically increase the absorption of topical treatments. Clinical research shows microneedling can increase the effectiveness of topical hair loss treatments by as much as 3× after 12 weeks. Used once weekly alongside the shampoo or Maxoxidil.

  • What is the returns policy?

    We offer a 90-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you've used the system consistently for 90 days and aren't satisfied with the results, contact our support team for a full refund — no questions asked.

  • Do you ship internationally?

    Yes — we ship worldwide. Free shipping available on qualifying orders. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout. Expedited options are available and orders typically arrive within 7–14 business days internationally.
